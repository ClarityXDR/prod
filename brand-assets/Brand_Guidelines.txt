ClarityXDR Brand Guidelines
================================

1. Logo
-------
Primary lockup (icon + wordmark) and simplified icon only. Maintain clear space equal to the icon height.

2. Color Palette
----------------
Lime Green: #7CDB46
Blaze Orange: #FF4500
Charcoal:   #0A0E23
Cool Light: #F5F7FA

3. Typography
-------------
Headings: Montserrat Bold
Body:      Open Sans Regular

4. Usage
--------
Use Lime Green for CTAs and highlights, Blaze Orange as accent. Prefer Charcoal text on Cool Light backgrounds.

5. Templates
------------
- MS Word letterhead (`ClarityXDR_Letterhead.dotx`)
- PowerPoint slide deck (`ClarityXDR_Template.pptx`)
